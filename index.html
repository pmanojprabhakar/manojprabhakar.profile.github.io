bio

Manoj Prabhakar is a Master's student in the Computer Science department at Virginia Tech. He joined Virginia Tech in Fall 2021 and currently working as a Graduate Research Assistant in the Experimental and Virtualized Systems (ELVES) lab. He earned his Electrical and Electronics Engineering bachelors at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, India. Before joining Virginia Tech, Manoj worked as a software engineer for four years at Cisco. He also worked at Nvidia and Microsoft as a software engineer intern. His research interests include Distributed Systems, Cloud Computing, Serverless Computing, and Machine Learning. Manoj likes to play ping pong and watch movies in his free time.
